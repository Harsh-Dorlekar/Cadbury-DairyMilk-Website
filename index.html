Cadbury Dairy Milk

Cadbury Dairy Milk is a brand of milk chocolate manufactured by Cadbury. It was introduced in the United Kingdom in 1905 and now consists of a wide range of products. It is the best selling chocolate bar in the UK and is the flagship product of Cadbury. It is one of the most popular chocolates in the world and is known for its smooth and creamy texture.
